Education Background

No.2 High School of East China Normal University

Founded in 1958, No. 2 High School is located in the Zhangjiang Hi-tech Park with convenient transportation, and covers an area of 10 ha with first-class basic establishment. The school is outstanding in international, continental, national, and regional science contests. It is also known as one of the best four public high schools in Shanghai

Shanghai Jiao Tong University

Shanghai Jiao Tong University is a major research university in Shanghai. Established on April 8, 1896 as Nanyang Public School by an imperial edict issued by the Guangxu Emperor since the 1930s. It is renowned as one of the oldest, most prestigious and selective universities in China. Shanghai Jiao Tong is a C9 League university and a Chinese Ministry of Education Class A Double First Class University.

University of Michigan

The University of Michigan is the oldest public research university in Ann Arbor, Michigan. It was founded in 1817, include more than 584 major buildings with a combined area of more than 34 million gross square feet. Michigan's comprehensive graduate program offers doctoral degrees in the humanities, social sciences, and STEM fields.
